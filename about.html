Our Story

MRE HUB was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality ready-to-eat meals to outdoor enthusiasts, survivalists, and busy professionals worldwide. What began as a passion project has grown into a global marketplace connecting customers with authentic MREs from over 30 countries.

Our founder, Elon Mask, spent years as an outdoor guide and recognized the need for better field rations that didn't compromise on taste or nutrition. Today, every product in our catalog meets strict quality standards and is personally tested by our team.

"We believe great food should be accessible anywhere - whether you're on a mountain peak or in your backyard. That's why we're committed to bringing you the world's best ready-to-eat meals."

— Elon Mask, Founder & CEO
